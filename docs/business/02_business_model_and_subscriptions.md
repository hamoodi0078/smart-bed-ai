## How the Business Makes Money

Smart Bed AI operates on a **hardware + recurring subscription** model. This is the same proven model used by companies like Peloton (exercise bikes + app), Eight Sleep (smart mattress + subscription), and Oura Ring (health ring + membership).

The logic is simple:
- **Hardware** generates a large one-time revenue when a customer buys
- **Subscriptions** generate steady, predictable monthly revenue from the same customer for years
- **Together**, they create a business that improves over time — each subscriber adds to a base of recurring income

The product is sold primarily **direct-to-consumer** (online and in select showrooms), with a separate **B2B channel** for hotels, clinics, and businesses.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Hardware Sales
The core product is the Smart Bed AI frame — a manufactured bed with embedded sensors, LEDs, and the Raspberry Pi AI brain. The customer purchases this once.

- **Consumer bed unit** — sold as a complete system including the bed frame, sensor kit, LED strips, speaker/microphone module, and power unit
- **Sensor upgrade kit** — sold separately for customers who want to add heart rate or room sensors later
- **Replacement parts** — LED strips, sensor pods, and speaker modules available for purchase after warranty

### 2. Subscription Fees (Monthly or Annual)
After buying the hardware, customers activate a subscription to unlock the AI and cloud-connected features. Without a subscription, the bed still works as a basic smart bed, but Danah's intelligence, Islamic features, reporting, and integrations are locked.

### 3. Add-On Purchases (Optional Upsells)
- **Premium Scene Packs** — custom mood lighting scenes (Ramadan Collection, Desert Night, Ocean Calm, etc.) sold as one-time purchases
- **Extended Warranty** — 2 or 3-year hardware protection plan
- **Priority Support** — faster response times and dedicated setup assistance
- **Family Plan Add-ons** — connect a second bed in the same household (e.g., a child's room)

### 4. B2B Licensing
Hotels, hospitals, and wellness centers purchase under a commercial license at a higher per-unit price, with a mandatory business subscription tier.

## Subscription Tiers

### Tier 1 — Free (Basic Mode)
*For customers who bought the hardware but are not ready to subscribe, or want to try before committing.*

**What's included:**
- Voice commands for basic controls (lights on/off, simple alarms)
- Manual sleep session logging (start/stop via app)
- Basic LED scene control
- App dashboard with current bed status
- One default wake-up alarm per day
- Standard Fajr notification (time-based, no smart wake logic)

**What's NOT included:**
- AI conversation and memory (Danah only responds with simple commands, no chat)
- Sleep analytics and scoring
- Islamic Mode (Ramadan, Tahajjud, Hadith, duaa routines)
- Partner Mode
- Guest Mode
- Integrations (Fitbit, Spotify, Google Calendar)
- Weekly health reports
- Adaptive routines and automation

**Why it exists:** It lets customers experience the hardware without a financial barrier, and creates a natural upgrade path once they see what they're missing.

**Price:** Free forever.

---

### Tier 2 — Standard (Most Popular)
*For individual users or couples who want the full Smart Bed AI experience at a fair price.*

**What's included:**
- Everything in Free, plus:
- **Full AI conversation** — Danah speaks, listens, remembers, and responds naturally
- **Long-term memory** — Danah remembers past conversations and builds context over time
- **3 AI personalities** — Guide (calm and spiritual), Coach (structured and goal-focused), Therapist (supportive and emotionally aware)
- **Sleep analytics and scoring** — nightly sleep score, trend charts, anomaly detection
- **Islamic Mode** — full Fajr wake-up (smart sunrise), all 5 prayer notifications, Ramadan mode (Suhoor alarm, fasting schedule), Tahajjud wake-up, daily Hadith, Islamic good night duaa
- **Adaptive wind-down routines** — automated bedtime rituals that adjust based on stress level, sleep history, and time
- **Smart sunrise alarm** — wakes you in your lightest sleep stage, not a jarring buzz
- **Partner Mode** — two independent profiles, individual wake-ups, separate Islamic settings
- **Spotify integration** — play sleep playlists, wind-down music, or morning tunes automatically
- **WhatsApp and push notifications** — sleep reminders, prayer alerts, Hadith
- **Basic weekly report** — sleep summary emailed weekly
- **Standard support** — WhatsApp chat support, in-app FAQ

**Why customers pay for this:** This is the "real Danah" experience. The AI, the Islamic features, the partner mode, and the sleep intelligence are all here. This tier converts the product from a smart light with an alarm into a genuine life companion.

**Estimated pricing:**
- Monthly: **~9–12 KWD / month** (approx. $30–40 USD)
- Annual: **~80–100 KWD / year** (approx. $265–330 USD) — roughly 30% savings

*Note: Prices are estimates for planning purposes. Final prices will be set based on market testing.*

### Tier 3 — Pro (Maximum Intelligence)
*For users who want the full health platform, B2B customers, and families who want multi-room capability.*

**What's included:**
- Everything in Standard, plus:
- **Advanced health analytics** — heart rate trends, SpO2 monitoring, room environment optimization (temperature, humidity alerts)
- **Fitbit / Garmin / wearable integration** — pull sleep stages, HRV, and recovery data from your wearable and combine it with bed sensor data
- **Full sleep intelligence engine** — ML-powered sleep prediction, bedtime drift alerts, weekly and monthly trend forecasting
- **Detailed PDF health reports** — full medical-grade sleep diary, shareable with a doctor or wellness coach
- **Family account** — up to 4 beds / users under one subscription
- **Guest Mode** — automatic privacy protection when a guest uses the bed
- **Gamification and achievements** — sleep streaks, Ramadan goals, challenge badges
- **Couple sleep health dashboard** — combined wellness score for partners
- **Google Calendar integration** — Danah adjusts wake times based on your calendar appointments
- **Premium scene packs included** — Ramadan Collection, seasonal scene libraries
- **Priority support** — dedicated onboarding call, faster response SLA, remote diagnostics
- **Early access** — new features before Standard users

**Why customers pay for this:** This tier is for serious users who treat sleep as an investment, and for B2B buyers who need multi-user management, detailed reports, and premium support. A hotel using Smart Bed AI needs Pro. A health-conscious professional who shares data with their doctor needs Pro.

**Estimated pricing:**
- Monthly: **~18–22 KWD / month** (approx. $60–73 USD)
- Annual: **~160–190 KWD / year** (approx. $530–630 USD) — roughly 30% savings
- B2B pricing: Custom quote per number of units (volume discount available)

*Note: Prices are estimates for planning purposes. Final prices will be set based on market testing.*

## What the Subscription Pays For

It is important to understand that subscriptions are not "pure profit." The monthly fee covers real operational costs:

| Cost Category | What It Covers |
|---|---|
| **AI usage costs** | Every conversation Danah has uses Deepgram (speech-to-text, text-to-speech) and Claude AI (intelligence). These are billed per usage by the API provider. |
| **Cloud servers** | User data, sleep analytics, reports, and app APIs run on cloud infrastructure (AWS or equivalent). More users = higher server cost. |
| **SMS / WhatsApp notifications** | Prayer time alerts, sleep reminders, and Suhoor messages are sent via messaging APIs (Twilio/WhatsApp Business). Each message has a small cost. |
| **Islamic content database** | Hadith content, prayer time calculations, Ramadan calendars, and Islamic knowledge base require curation and maintenance. |
| **Mobile app updates** | The Flutter app on iOS and Android requires ongoing development, bug fixes, and new features. |
| **Customer support** | WhatsApp chat agents, onboarding calls, and technical support are paid staff. |
| **Hardware warranty** | A portion of subscription revenue is reserved for hardware replacement costs during the warranty period. |

**At Standard tier**, the goal is for subscription revenue to cover AI + cloud costs with a margin remaining for operations and growth.

**At Pro tier**, the higher price point also covers the more intensive API usage (detailed health analytics, integrations, priority support) and creates a stronger profit margin.

## Unit Economics Summary

For each customer who buys hardware and subscribes at Standard tier:

- **One-time hardware revenue:** ~500 KWD
- **Annual subscription revenue:** ~90 KWD
- **Estimated year 1 value of one customer:** ~590 KWD
- **Estimated year 3 value (if retained):** ~770 KWD (hardware + 3 years subscription)

Customer retention is critical. A customer who subscribes for 3 years is worth 50% more than one who subscribes for 1 year. This is why the product experience — especially the Islamic features, the AI memory, and the genuine daily value — must be excellent enough to prevent churn.

---

## Why This Business Model Works Long-Term

1. **Recurring revenue compounds.** 100 customers in year 1 who stay through year 3 = a growing, predictable income base without needing to acquire 100 new customers every year.

2. **Hardware anchors the customer.** Once someone has the physical bed in their home, they are unlikely to stop the subscription — the bed loses much of its value without it.

3. **Islamic features create loyalty.** Features like Fajr wake-up, Ramadan mode, and Tahajjud are deeply personal and meaningful. Customers who use these features have strong emotional ties to the product.

4. **The data improves over time.** The longer a customer uses the system, the better Danah knows them, the more useful she becomes, and the more reluctant the customer is to leave. This is a natural retention engine.

---

*Consistent subscription revenue + hardware anchor + emotional loyalty = a business that gets stronger every year.*
