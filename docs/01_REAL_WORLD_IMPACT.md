## What Is This Project?

Smart Bed AI is a **smart bed system** that makes your sleep better and your life easier. Think of it like having a caring personal assistant that lives inside your bed. Her name is **Danah**.

Danah can:
- **Talk to you** using voice — you speak, she listens and responds
- **Track your sleep** — she knows when you fall asleep, when you wake up, and how well you slept
- **Control the lights** around your bed — changing colors, brightness, and mood automatically
- **Monitor your health** — reading your heart rate, body temperature, and room humidity
- **Help you with Islamic worship** — prayer times, Fajr wake-up, Ramadan schedules, daily Hadith
- **Connect to your phone** — a mobile app gives you full control from anywhere

## Who Is This For?

### 1. Anyone Who Struggles With Sleep
Millions of people worldwide have poor sleep. They go to bed too late, wake up tired, or toss and turn all night. Smart Bed AI helps them by:

- **Tracking their sleep patterns** over weeks and months
- **Detecting bad habits** — like going to bed at different times every night (called "bedtime drift")
- **Creating a wind-down routine** — 45 minutes before bed, the lights slowly dim, calming sounds play, and Danah guides a breathing exercise
- **Waking them gently** — instead of a loud alarm, the lights gradually brighten like a sunrise, timed to match their sleep cycle so they wake up refreshed instead of groggy

**Real impact:** Studies show consistent sleep schedules and gentle waking can improve sleep quality by 30-40%. This system automates that.

### 2. Muslim Families
This is the first smart bed system in the world with deep Islamic features:

- **Fajr wake-up** — the bed knows exactly when Fajr prayer is in your city. 20 minutes before, it starts a gentle light increase so you wake naturally and peacefully for prayer
- **Prayer time notifications** — you get a reminder for each of the 5 daily prayers
- **Tahajjud automation** — if you want to pray the night prayer, the bed calculates the last third of the night and wakes you softly
- **Ramadan mode** — during Ramadan, the bed wakes you for Suhoor (pre-dawn meal) and tracks your fasting schedule. It reduces AI interactions during fasting hours out of respect
- **Daily Hadith** — every day, you receive a Hadith (saying of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ) via notification and voice
- **Islamic good night** — Danah can say the Sunnah dua before sleep: "بِسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا"

**Real impact:** No other smart home product in the world serves Muslim daily life like this. It turns a bed into a worship companion.

### 3. Couples
Most smart bed products treat the bed as one person's. This system supports **partner mode**:

- **Two separate profiles** — each partner has their own sleep preferences, wake time, and health data
- **Dual pressure detection** — the system knows which side of the bed each person is on
- **Individual wake** — one partner can be woken gently (light on their side only) without disturbing the other
- **Couple sleep goals** — track your combined sleep health as a couple

**Real impact:** 60% of couples report their partner's sleep habits affect their own. This solves that.

### 4. People With Anxiety or Stress
Danah has a **Therapist personality** specifically designed for emotional support:

- **Daily check-ins** — "How was your stress level today?" and "What was one good thing that happened today?"
- **Guided breathing** — the 4-7-8 breathing technique: breathe in for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8. The LED lights sync with the breathing rhythm
- **Pattern detection** — if you have 3+ bad nights in a week, Danah notices and says: "I've noticed a pattern of difficult sleep this week. Let's talk about what might be affecting you."
- **Calming affirmations** — "I release today and allow myself to rest without guilt."
- **Crisis detection** — if Danah detects signs of severe distress, she provides emergency resources

**Real impact:** Cognitive behavioral techniques for insomnia (CBT-I) are recommended by doctors as first-line treatment. This system delivers some of those techniques automatically.

### 5. Health-Conscious Users
The system connects to real health devices:

- **Heart rate and SpO2 monitoring** — a MAX30102 sensor near the bed measures heart rate and blood oxygen while you sleep
- **Room environment** — a DHT22 sensor tracks temperature and humidity. The bed can warn you if the room is too hot or too dry for good sleep (ideal: 18-22°C, 40-60% humidity)
- **Fitbit / Garmin integration** — if you wear a fitness tracker, the system pulls your sleep stages, HRV, steps, and body battery data and combines it with the bed's own sensors
- **Weekly health reports** — a PDF report is generated every week showing your sleep trends, heart rate patterns, and improvement suggestions. This can be shared with a doctor

**Real impact:** Sleep disorders affect 1 in 3 adults. Many go undiagnosed because people don't track their sleep properly. This system creates a medical-grade sleep diary automatically.

### 6. Guests
When someone else sleeps in your bed (a guest, family member), the system:

- **Auto-detects** unusual pressure patterns (different body weight/position)
- **Activates guest mode** — switches to basic features only
- **Protects your privacy** — hides your personal data, sleep history, and AI conversation memory
- **Notifies the owner** that guest mode was activated

**Real impact:** Privacy protection that most smart home products don't even think about.

## The Technology That Makes This Possible

### Voice That Feels Natural
- You say "Hey Smart Bed" and Danah wakes up
- She converts your voice to text using Deepgram (one of the world's best speech engines)
- She thinks using Claude AI (one of the most advanced AI models)
- She speaks back using Deepgram's natural-sounding voice
- If you interrupt her while she's talking, she stops and listens (called "barge-in")
- If you're both talking at the same time, she knows to ignore her own voice playing back through the speaker (echo cancellation)

### Memory That Lasts
Unlike most AI assistants that forget everything after each conversation:
- Danah remembers your past conversations
- She knows what you talked about last week
- She uses this memory to give contextual, personal responses
- Example: "Last time you mentioned your exam stress. How did that go?"

### Lighting That Adapts
- 180 LED lights around the bed (120 ambient + 60 status)
- Colors change based on time of day (warm at night, cool in morning — matching your circadian rhythm)
- Colors change based on weather (warmer tones on cold days, cooler on hot days)
- Colors sync with breathing exercises
- Custom scenes you can create and save

### Health Monitoring That Works While You Sleep
- Pressure pad knows when you're in bed, and how much you move
- Heart rate sensor tracks your resting heart rate and blood oxygen
- Temperature sensor ensures your room is optimal
- All data feeds into the sleep score algorithm
